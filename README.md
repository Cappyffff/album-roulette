# 100 Albums to Listen To 🎵

One person. One chart of 100 albums. One random album a day, rated out of 100.

## How it works

Every day the site spins a slot-machine reel and lands on a random album from
the chart — no repeats, ever. After a full listen it gets an official rating
out of 100 and some thoughts. Slowly but surely, all 100 get done.

## What you can do here

- **Today** — see what album is currently up. Once you've listened to it,
  scroll down and drop your own review: your name, your score out of 100, and
  what you thought. Everyone's welcome to weigh in.
- **Past → History** — every album rolled so far, day by day, with ratings
  and comments.
- **Past → Leaderboard** — all finished albums ranked by their official
  score. 🥇🥈🥉
- **Past → The Chart** — the full 10×10 grid of all 100 albums. Finished ones
  are lit up with their score; the rest are waiting their turn. Tap any album
  to see its day, scores, and everyone's reviews.
- **Thoughts** — every review anyone has left, grouped by album.

## Know a number between 1 and 100?

Sometimes the daily album isn't random — it's a number someone suggested.
Pick a number 1–100, check the chart to see if it's still unclaimed, and pass
it along. That album might be next.

## The fine print

The spin, the official ratings, and review moderation are locked to the list
keeper. Reviews are open to everyone — be yourself, put your name on it.
